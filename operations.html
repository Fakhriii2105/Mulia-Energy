Our Operations

Strategic assets across South Kalimantan — from world-class coal reserves to integrated logistics and rehabilitation projects.

Main Mining Hub

Our flagship operation in the Kalimantan Basin delivers premium reserves with optimized production efficiency and global-standard safety practices. Direct access to ports ensures a reliable supply chain for international partners.

Integrated Logistics

We own and operate hauling roads, river barges, and port facilities, ensuring end-to-end control from mine to market. This integration reduces cost, improves delivery speed, and enhances investor confidence in our supply reliability.

Processing & Handling

Advanced processing plants and handling systems enable efficient coal sizing, blending, and stockpiling. Stringent protocols safeguard workforce safety and minimize environmental footprint.

Land Reclamation

Our rehabilitation programs restore mined areas into productive lands, supporting biodiversity and local community livelihoods. This long-term commitment strengthens both environmental and social value creation.
